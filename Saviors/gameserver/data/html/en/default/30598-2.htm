Newbie Guide:
To start, determine its primary purpose. Suppose that you are now just a young swordsman, but in the future can become the lord or even a king!
The first thing you have to get used to the new weapons and armor. No matter who you want to be - a killer of dragons or peacemaker - will have to start from scratch.
Begin to train on wolves in the surrounding countryside. From monsters will drop items, some may be helpful. The rest are sold in retail shops.
