Newbie Guide:
Start with Red Keltir's or Gray Wolf's in the surrounding countryside. But be careful with the wolves.
Upon reaching level 6 go to the south-west or south-east and try to hunt Kaboo Orc's. But remember that the orcs attacking those who offend their comrades.
If you select a monster in the top of the screen will appear the name, the color of which will tell you how strong the monster. It is better to choose those monsters whose names are marked with white or blue. Opponents, whose name is highlighted in yellow or red, is much stronger than you. Circumvention of such party.
And also try to fight alongside the shadow of the Tree of Life. This allows you to quickly restore energy.
