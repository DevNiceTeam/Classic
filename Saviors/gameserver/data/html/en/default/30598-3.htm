Newbie Guide:
Do you still wear the armor and weapons that you have given at the Cedric's Training Hall?
Well, the first time can not be choosers. To get new gear, you need to hunt monsters. The first acquisitions are your trophies. If you're lucky, pretty soon you got the Cloth Shoes or Apprentice's Shoes and Cloth Cap.
At the beginning of training you were given two weapons. Dagger and Squire's Sword. You probably already noticed how much they differ from each other. Dagger comfortable and well in the hand, but he has low accuracy. Squire's Sword (One-handed Sword) slower dagger, but more likely to cause serious damage to the enemy. You still have time to choose weapons.It depends on all further training.
