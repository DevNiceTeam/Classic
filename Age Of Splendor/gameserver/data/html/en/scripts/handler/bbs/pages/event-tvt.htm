About event:
Players are randomly divided into two teams - red and blue.
The award winning team players receive survivors.
The goal - to defeat the opposing team. Event held at the Coliseum.
You may use any items. Buffs are not removed before the fight.
Given 3 minutes to register, 1 minute on teleportation, 5 minutes of the fight.
If the winner will be determined before the battle ends automatically.
At the end of the event, all participants are resurrected, cured, and teleport back.
In case of death, the experience is not lost.
