Newbie Guide:
In this village, many in need of your help. Do not hesitate to talk to someone. When you start up a dialogue between, click on the option Quest. Then you probably will be asked about the service.
Locals at a glance can determine with whom they deal. Those who need help will make sure not to expose you to danger because of your inexperience.
Assignments are of two kinds. Some can be done only once, but there are repetitive, that can be performed several times.
