Newbie Guide:
Um, you still go with conventional weapons and armor, obtained in a guild?
Well, the first time can not be choosers. Focus on the hunt and hope for good luck. The first acquisitions are your trophies. If you're lucky, pretty soon you got the Cloth Shoes or Apprentice's Shoes and Cloth Cap.
The guild you were given two weapons. The dagger and a blunt. Hopefully, you have already tried both types, and understand the difference between them. The dagger attack quickly, but rarely hits the target, but has a high chance of critical attack. Blunt much slower dagger, but misses the target consistently. You still have time to choose weapons. It depends on all further training.
