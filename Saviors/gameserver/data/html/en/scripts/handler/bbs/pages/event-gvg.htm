About event:
Tournament Group vs Group will move your group in a dungeon with another random group submitted. The main objective of both groups - to collect a greater number of points than the opponent team.
Designed for groups consisting of 6 or more participants. The leader of the group must apply during the registration period, the group must meet the following requirements trunira:
The group should not be to channel command (Command Channel)
Each member of the group must be in the game
Each group member should not leave the group
The group must consist of a minimum of 6 participants
The group leader should not change after filing
Each member of the group should be level 80-85
Each group member should not use mounts after filing
Each group member should not take part in a duel after filing
Each group member should not participate in other after application of Event
Each member of the group do not need to register or participate in the Olympics after filing
The group should not be in the Dimensional Rift or Delusion Chamber
Any member of the group can not have a cursed weapon
The whole team should be in a peaceful area during application and until the end of the registration
