Newbie Guide:
Hm ... You as a novice, still go to the weapons and armor, obtained by the Priests of the Flame?
Well, the first time can not be choosers. Focus on the hunt and hope for good luck. The first acquisitions are your trophies. If you're lucky, pretty soon you got the Cloth Shoes or Apprentice's Shoes and Cloth Cap.
The guild you were given two weapons. Sword Squire and Training Fist. You probably already tried them and realized that the sword and brass knuckles different in their characteristics. Training Fist allow inflict two hits instead of one. They put on both hands, so you can not use a shield. Squire's Sword (one-handed) has a slower rate of attack, but it strikes more accurate. You still have time to choose weapons. It depends on all further training.
