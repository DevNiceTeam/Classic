Newbie Guide:
You, as a beginner, probably still using weapons and armor, obtained in the Temple of Shilen.
Well, the first time can not be choosers. To get new gear, you need to hunt monsters. If you're lucky, you can get such basic armor as Cloth Shoes, Apprentice's Shoes or Cloth Cap.
At the beginning of training you were given two weapons. Dagger and Squire's Sword. Dagger comfortable and well in the hand, but he has low accuracy. Squire's Sword (One-handed Sword) slower dagger, but more likely to cause serious damage to the enemy. You still have time to choose weapons.It depends on all further training.
