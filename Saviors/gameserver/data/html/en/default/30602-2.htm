Newbie Guide:
To start, determine its primary purpose. Now the kingdom of Aden is a war, it is in a difficult situation. But you know that it is in times of discord appear excellent opportunities. You are still an inexperienced soldier, but someday you will cope with the chaos that is going on around, and become orc chieftain.
The first thing you have to get used to the new weapons and armor. No matter who you want to be - the greatest killer of dragons and orc chieftain - you should start small.
Start with battles with the wolves in the surrounding countryside. From monsters will drop items, some may be helpful. The rest are sold in retail shops.
